THE TRIGGER: FY2027 Budget Proposal

Eliminate ALL competitive & recreational gymnastics programs. Close Barcroft Sports & Fitness Center (28,000 sq. ft.). Effective May 16, 2026. No community input. County's own budget survey never asked about it.
